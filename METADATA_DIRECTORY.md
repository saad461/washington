## 2. Dynamic Content Patterns

### A. Blog Posts (`/blog/[slug]`)
Pulls unique data from `data/blogs.ts`.
- **Title Pattern:** `[post.metaTitle] | WSCSS`
- **Description Pattern:** `[post.metaDescription]`

### B. Glossary Terms (`/glossary/[slug]`)
Pulls unique data from `data/glossary.ts`.
- **Title Pattern:** `[term.metaTitle] | WSCSS` (e.g. "Self-Support Reserve (SSR) in WA 2026")
- **Description Pattern:** `[term.metaDescription]` (A clear definition of the specific legal term)

### C. County Sub-pages (`/washington-courts/[county]`)
Covers all 39 Washington counties.
- **Title Pattern:** `[County Name] Child Support 2026 | WSCSS`
- **Description Pattern:** `Calculate 2026 child support in [County Name], WA. Courthouse address, filing fee, SSR rules ($2,394), and filing guides under RCW 26.19.`

### D. Programmatic Income Pages (`/[slug]`)
Thousands of combinations (e.g. `/king-county-income-5000-2-children`).
- **Title Pattern:** `Child Support $[Income] – [County], WA ([Children Label]) | 2026 | WSCSS`
- **Description Pattern:** `2026 child support for $[Income]/mo income and [Children Label] in [County], WA. Presumptive: $[Amount]/mo. Includes SSR and RCW 26.19 rules.`
